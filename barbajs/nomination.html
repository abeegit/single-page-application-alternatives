Do you want to the be the Tanishq Man? Here are a few tips on how you can submit a winning entry.

  • 1. Accessorise!

    It's all about the accessories. Make sure you are sporting your favourite accessories in every photograph.

  • 2. Lighting

    A picture with the perfect lighting is sure to make a winning entry. Ensure all your submissions are well lit!

  • 3. Description

    Your description should capture the essence of what makes you a Tanishq Man. Make sure you put those 280 characters to good use.

  • 4. Have fun!

    The Tanishq Man knows how to have a good time. Make sure you are having fun in the pictures you upload.

  • 5. What's in the background?

    A good picture always tells a story in the background. Make sure there is something going on there, not just a plain white wall.
